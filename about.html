Our Story

Welcome to MooMuffin Acres, a family-owned and operated farm nestled in the beautiful countryside. Our journey began in 2022 with a simple dream: to create a sustainable, happy place where animals thrive and fresh, healthy produce grows.

After many years working in related industries, farming felt like the next logical step for our family. What started as a small homestead has grown into the farm you see today, but our core values remain the same â€“ we believe in transparency, quality, and putting family first in everything we do.

Our work on the farm is truly a labor of love, and we're proud to raise quality livestock and grow top-notch produce for our local community. Every day brings new adventures and learning experiences as we continue to grow our farm family.

Our mission is to nurture happy animals, grow nutritious food, and foster connections between people and the land that feeds them. We strive to operate our farm with integrity, sustainability, and joy in everything we do.

Our Journey

2022

Farm Foundation

We purchased our 60-acre property and began the exciting journey of transforming it into the farm of our dreams. Our first additions were a small flock of chickens and a vegetable garden.

2022

First Animals

We welcomed our first family of miniature highland cattle to the farm, beginning our focus on raising happy, healthy animals in a natural environment.

2023

Farm Expansion

With the addition of miniature donkeys, ducks, and goats, our farm family continued to grow. We also expanded our produce offerings with more varieties of fruits and vegetables.

2023

Farm Stand Opens

We opened our farm stand to share our fresh eggs, seasonal produce, and other farm products with the local community, creating connections with our neighbors.

2024

Community Programs

We began offering farm tours and educational programs to share our knowledge and passion for sustainable farming with visitors of all ages.

2025

Looking Ahead

As we continue to grow, we're excited about future plans including expanded animal breeding programs, new produce varieties, and more opportunities to connect with our community.

Our Core Values

Transparency

Ask us anything! We work together as a family to nurture our animals, which means we're in a position to tell you anything you want to know about how they're being raised. We believe in open, honest communication with our customers and community.

Family-Owned

Everything we do here is a family affair. We're proud to be a family-owned and operated homestead focused on sustainable practices. Our children are involved in daily farm operations, learning valuable life skills and a strong work ethic.

Quality

From the best breeding stock to feed choices to the nurturing care we provide, quality is at the forefront of everything at our farm. We never cut corners and are committed to producing the best products possible for our customers.
