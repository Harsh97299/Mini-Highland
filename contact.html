Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some common questions we receive. If you don't see your question here, please contact us!

Do you offer farm tours?

Yes, we offer guided tours of our farm by appointment. Tours typically last 1-2 hours and include visits with our animals, a walk through our gardens, and time at our farm shop. We charge $10 per person (children under 5 are free). You can schedule a tour by filling out our contact form or calling us directly.

Can I purchase animals from your farm?

Yes, we occasionally have Miniature Highland cattle and other animals available for sale. All our animals are raised with love and care, and we ensure they go to homes where they'll continue to receive the same quality of life. Please visit our Animals page to see current availability or contact us for more information about purchasing an animal.

Do you ship your farm products?

Most of our farm products must be picked up in person at our farm shop during regular hours. However, we do offer shipping for select non-perishable items like honey, preserves, and gift boxes. Fresh produce and eggs are available for local pickup only to ensure the highest quality.

Can I host an event at your farm?

We do allow certain types of small events at our farm, including educational workshops, photography sessions, and small gatherings. Due to space limitations and our focus on the animals' well-being, we evaluate each request individually. Please contact us with details about your proposed event, and we'll let you know if it's something we can accommodate.

Do you offer farm-to-table dinners or events?

Yes! During the summer months (June-August), we host monthly farm-to-table dinners featuring produce from our gardens and locally sourced meats and cheeses. These dinners are prepared by local chefs and served in our garden setting. Tickets must be purchased in advance and typically sell out quickly. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when tickets become available.
